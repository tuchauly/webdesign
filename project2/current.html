Such a Menace

Now a full-fledged adult doggie, Lucky has become a menace and embraced his menace nature.

Not only does he protect his humans from the mailman, but he goes on hour-long adventures, chase squirels and mark his territory. He also fully embraced his only-child status (he gets upset if we mention getting him a brother) thorugh a plethora of toys, a slection of treats, and a personal heater.

Regardless, we all love Sir Luck!
